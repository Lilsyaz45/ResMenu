OUR STORY

Mojo Restaurant began its journey in the year 2000 with a simple dream: to bring the finest taste of French dining to Cambodia. From the very beginning, the founders believed that extraordinary food starts with extraordinary ingredients. That is why Mojo has always searched the world for the highest-quality produce, choosing only the best—no matter where it comes from.
